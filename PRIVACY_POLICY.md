# PulaCash — Privacy Policy

**Effective date: 2026-06-28 · Jurisdiction: Republic of Botswana**

PulaCash ("we", "us") provides short-term emergency microloans to verified students
enrolled at recognised institutions in Botswana. This Privacy Policy explains how we
collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. We process personal data
in accordance with the **Botswana Data Protection Act, 2018**. By using PulaCash you
consent to the practices described here.

## 1. Information we collect
- **Account details:** full name, institutional student email, and password (stored
  only as a salted **scrypt** hash — never in plain text and never recoverable).
- **Profile details:** institution, student number, and phone number.
- **Identity document:** the student ID image/PDF you upload for verification.
- **Payment details:** processed through our payment providers (e.g. your
  mobile-money number) to disburse loans and collect repayments and membership fees.
- **Service records:** loan applications, disbursements, repayments, membership, and
  your reliability score, generated through your use of PulaCash.

## 2. How we use your information
To verify that you are an enrolled student; to assess and process loan applications;
to manage repayments; to calculate your reliability score; to prevent fraud and
abuse; to comply with legal and regulatory obligations; and to provide support.

## 3. Legal basis & consent
We process your data to perform our contract with you (providing loans), to comply
with legal obligations, for our legitimate interest in preventing fraud, and on the
basis of the consent you give when registering and uploading identity documents.

## 4. Storage & security
Passwords are stored only as scrypt hashes. Session tokens are stored only as SHA-256
hashes and expire automatically. Identity documents are held in access-controlled
storage. We apply technical and organisational measures to protect your data, though
no system is perfectly secure.

## 5. Sharing & disclosure
We do **not** sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers who
help operate PulaCash (e.g. secure storage and transactional email), and with
regulators or authorities where required by law, including the **Non-Bank Financial
Institutions Regulatory Authority (NBFIRA)**.

## 6. Data retention
We keep your data for as long as your account is active and as required to meet legal,
regulatory, and audit obligations relating to lending. When no longer required, data
is deleted or anonymised.

## 7. Your rights
Subject to the Botswana Data Protection Act, 2018, you may request access to,
correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and may withdraw consent. You may
also request deletion of your account in-app. To exercise these rights, contact us.

## 8. Children
PulaCash is intended only for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data
from anyone under 18.

## 9. Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated
in-app.
